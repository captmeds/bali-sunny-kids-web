# Join Our Family

We are delighted that you're considering **Bali Sunny Kids** for your child's early education. Our enrollment process is designed to be simple and welcoming.

## How to Enroll

1.  **Book a Tour:** Visit us in Ubud, tour our rooms, and meet our passionate educators. We recommend visiting during morning sessions to see our environment in action.
2.  **Submit Inquiry:** Fill out our basic inquiry form or contact us via WhatsApp to check availability for your child's age group.
3.  **Complete Application:** We will provide an enrollment pack with all the necessary forms.
4.  **Confirm Placement:** Once the forms are returned and the initial fee is paid, your child's spot is secured.

## Required Documents

To complete the enrollment, you will need:

*   Child's Birth Certificate or Passport.
*   Parents' ID (KTP or Passport).
*   Recent immunization records.
*   Two passport-sized photos of the child.
