# Our Story

Operating in the heart of Ubud, Gianyar, since **2000**, Bali Sunny Kids has been a cornerstone of early childhood education for over two decades. We provide quality kindergarten programs for 3-year-old and 4-year-old groups, focusing on the unique development of every child.

## Our Philosophy

We believe that the universe is one great kindergarten. Our primary focus is the **wellbeing of every child**. We pay close attention to helping them grow into happy, healthy, and confident individuals who are ready for their next big step into primary school.

## Our Vision

We acknowledge that all members of our community—including children, families, and teachers—have rights. We strive to create a space where every interaction is based on:

*   **Trust and Safety:** Ensuring a secure environment for exploration.
*   **Playful Learning:** Where curiosity is the main driver of education.
*   **Community:** Building strong bonds between parents and educators.
*   **Sustainability:** Teaching kids to respect the beautiful Bali environment they grow up in.

## Meet Our Educators

Our team consists of passionate educators who specialize in early childhood development. With a low teacher-to-child ratio, we ensure that your child receives the individual attention they deserve.
