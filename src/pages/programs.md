# Educational Programs

At Bali Sunny Kids, we understand that children learn best through play and exploration. Our programs are designed to be developmentally appropriate, ensuring each child progresses at their own pace.

## 3-Year-Old Group
**Developing Foundations**

This program focuses on transitioning from home to a social learning environment. We emphasize:

*   **Social & Emotional Skills:** Learning to play with others, sharing, and expressing feelings.
*   **Creative Expression:** Painting, music, and basic storytelling.
*   **Physical Development:** Fine motor skills (drawing, blocks) and gross motor skills (outdoor games).
*   **Language Development:** Introducing basic vocabulary and communication through songs and rhymes.

## 4-Year-Old Group
**Preparing for Success**

Our 4-year-old program builds on the foundations and starts preparing children for primary school. Highlights include:

*   **Early Literacy & Numeracy:** Recognizing letters, sounds, and numbers through "Magic Number" classroom games.
*   **Problem Solving:** Building complex structures and solving simple puzzles.
*   **Independence:** Encouraging self-help skills like dressing and tidying up.
*   **Environmental Awareness:** Learning about the world around us, focusing on Bali's unique nature.
